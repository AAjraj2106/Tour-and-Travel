Nainital

Known as the "Lake District of India", Nainital is a scenic hill station featuring a beautiful lake, temples, and colonial buildings.

Mussoorie

Mussoorie, the Queen of Hills, offers stunning views of the Himalayas, waterfalls, and colonial charm. Ideal for a peaceful getaway.

Rishikesh

Adventure capital and spiritual center of India. Experience yoga, Ganga aarti, and thrilling river rafting in Rishikesh.

Kedarnath

One of the holiest Hindu temples, Kedarnath is nestled in the Garhwal Himalayas, attracting thousands of pilgrims each year.
